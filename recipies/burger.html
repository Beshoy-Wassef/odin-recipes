Burger

Description

This is a standard burger dish

Ingredients

  • Frozen burger patties
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Burger buns
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Place frozen patty on frying pan and set heat to high
  2. After 5 minutes, flip the patty and let it set for another 5 minutes
  3. Place cheese slice on the patty and close the pan with lid
  4. After 1-2 minutes take out the patty and place on a plate
  5. Prepare the buns by putting ketchup on the top and bottom bun first then all the other toppings on the bottom bun
  6. Place the burger in the bun and enjoy
