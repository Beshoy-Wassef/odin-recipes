Scrambled Eggs

Home

Description

This is the scrambled eggs dish

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 5g Butter
  • Frying pan
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare a pan and lightly melt some butter on it over medium heat.
  2. After the butter has melted, go ahead and crack 3 eggs into the pan.
  3. Lightly and continuesly stir the pan with a spatula, you will notice that the eggs will begin to solidify.
  4. Salt and pepper the eggs at this stage.
  5. After the eggs are solid but still lightly runny, empty them onto a plate
