Hot Chocolate

Description

This is the hot chocolate drink in New York style

Ingredients

  • Bittersweet chocolate
  • Semi-sweet chocolate
  • Coco powder (Dutch Processed)
  • Whole milk
  • Heavy Cream
  • Cornstarch
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan, pour your milk and turn up the heat to med-high
  2. Add the cornstarch
  3. Add the heavy cream
  4. Add the sugar (optional to taste) and whisk to combine
  5. Add the coco powder
  6. Turn off the heat and add the chocolate and vanilla
  7. Keep mixing until all the chocolate is melted and mixed into the milk
  8. Once the hot chocolate thickens, it is ready to serve
